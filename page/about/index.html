About me

Although I started my bachelor’s in Psychology, I developed a keen interest in statistics already in the first few months of my study. After focussing my bachelor’s as much on statistics as possible, including a bachelor’s thesis on multivariate methods, I started the master’s program Methodology and Statistics for the Behavioural, Biomedical and Social Sciences at Utrecht University. Despite my background in Psychology, I am specifically interested in biomedical statistics as it provides amazingly interesting data and great possibilities to collaborate with people from various backgrounds.
